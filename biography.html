Jeremy Bradley Earl is a NY-based visual artist and musician in
the psychedelic folk rock band Woods. His drawings and collages
evoke shamanic illustrations for magical campfire folklore with an
easy, loose line work that endears and maintains an allure of mystery.
His work has been featured on countless album and tape covers
for his own record label, Woodsist / Fuck It Tapes, and is responsible
for creating an iconic aesthetic that is synonymous with the visual
language of contemporary homegrown / indie / folk / cassette culture.
